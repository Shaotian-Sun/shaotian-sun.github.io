## Current Research

### Conitional Exponential Directed Last-Passage Percolation

I am working with **Professor Jinho Baik** on directed last-passage percolation with Rishi. Our current work focuses on fluctuation behavior with step and flat initial conditions, and extensions to more general boundary geometries conditional on an upper large deviation event.

### High-Dimensional Statistical Inference

Under the guidance of **Professor Gongjun Xu**, I am studying identifiability, local strong convexity, consistency, and asymptotic inference for Blade-Chest-Inner Model, an inransitive pairwise comparison model.
